Site Name

Uganda Tourism and Culture

This name was selected because the website promotes Uganda's tourism attractions, cultural heritage, wildlife, traditional foods, and travel destinations. It clearly communicates the purpose of the site.

Optional Domain: ugandatourismculture.org

Site Purpose

The website provides information about Uganda's tourist attractions, national parks, wildlife, cultural traditions, kingdoms, historical sites, traditional foods, and travel tips. It serves as a guide for both local and international visitors who want to explore Uganda.

Scenarios

  • What are the best national parks and wildlife reserves to visit in Uganda?
  • Which cultural traditions and kingdoms can tourists explore?
  • What traditional Ugandan foods should visitors try?
  • What travel tips should tourists know before visiting Uganda?

Color Schema

Forest Green (#2E8B57) - Used for headings, navigation, and footer sections.

Golden Yellow (#F4B400) - Used for highlights, buttons, and accents.

White (#FFFFFF) - Used for page backgrounds and content areas.

Typography

Montserrat - Used for headings and section titles.

Open Sans - Used for body text and paragraphs.
